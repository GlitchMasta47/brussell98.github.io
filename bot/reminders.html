Creating a New Reminder

The command to set a reminder uses the follwing format: ]remindme <the reminder here> in <the ammount of time here>


For an example fill in the boxes below

in days hours minutes & seconds

List Your Reminders

This will tell you what your current reminders are and when they are set for.

]remindme list


Example response: 

Here are your reminders:
watch that thing @ Sun, 24 Apr 2016 05:23:50 GMT
add that command @ Sat, 23 Apr 2016 08:24:13 GMT

Remove a Reminder

Use this if you want to delete a reminder that you set.

]remindme remove <the reminder>
